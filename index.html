Cipher Of Anatomy

Welcome to the home of Cipher Of Anatomy, my novel that is free for the world to enjoy. My name is Karl Bailey, this website is my first attempt of pushing myself to truly share my writing. I've been writing for as long as I can remember, but in recent years I found that I began to write less and less. Now I want to make an attempt to complete a work that I can be proud of, I hope that you will join me as I progress on my journey with Cipher Of Anatomy. A new chapter will be added every two weeks, available to read on the site or download as a PDF for free. I am building this story as I go so of course feel free to contact me, let me know what you think, suggest changes I should make, help me to build this novel into something everyone can enjoy.

Cipher Of Anatomy

Four years after dectective Christain Wake failed to apprehend the serial killer Harold Kelly. A body is found and it appears that he may be back to finish off the job he started. Wake is no longer the man he once was, having lost everything and living in the bottom of a bottle will he be able to pull himself out? Is this the push he needs? Or will this be the final nail in the coffin for detective Christian Wake?

Point Of View

Set four years prior to Cipher Of Anatomy, this is Point Of View. The start of Harold Kelly's killing spree. The story is told from the different perspectives of each character as it unfolds, a short novella to give more insight into the characters. New sections may be added to this less frequently than Cipher Of Anatomy, keep an eye on the updates section to find out when they will be released.
