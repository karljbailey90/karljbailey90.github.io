Nephew & nieces.
Drawn from 3 photos.

Was asked if I could drawn a picture of my nephew and two nieces. Rather than going for photo realism I took some liberties and drew it in the style I favour the most and added the tattoo style butterflies and twisties into it to tie the three pictures together.
